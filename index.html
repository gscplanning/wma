Disclaimer

This map was created as part of the visioning process for the expansion of equestrian and multi-modal travel in Scott County. The potential paths shown in this map do not necessarily represent planned paths, but rather serve as jumping off points for further conversations. If you have questions, comments, or concerns, feel free to contact the Georgetown-Scott County Planning Commission at email@gscplanning.com or (502) 867-3701.
